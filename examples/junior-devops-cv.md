## Why this example works

- The target role is obvious.
- The skills section is grouped rather than presented as a random list.
- Each major skill appears again inside evidence.
- Projects explain implementation, not only features.
- It does not claim production scale or fake business metrics.
- “Fundamentals” is used where the depth is introductory.
- Team contribution is described without taking credit for all work.
